1.0 THE VISION: Music Set Free
For decades, the music industry has been a "Black Box." Money goes in, but creators rarely know where it goes or why they are paid so little. Fans have no say in what gets played. Great songs die because of gatekeepers.

Otto Music is the Glass Box.

We are building a complete ecosystem where Human Artistry and AI Innovation live in harmony. Whether you are a classical cellist, a bedroom remixer using AI tools, or a fan who loves discovering new sounds, Otto is your home.

We are not just a streaming platform. We are a Digital Nation for Sound, governed by the people who create and consume music.

2.0 THE CREATOR SPECTRUM: A Home for All
In the Otto ecosystem, there is no "hierarchy" of creation. We value the soul of the human and the speed of the machine equally.

2.1 The Traditional Creator (The Human Core)
For the instrumentalist, the vocalist, the songwriter. You bring the raw emotion. We bring the protection.

Upload & Protect: When you upload a track created entirely by human hands, it is stamped with a "Human Origin" badge on the blockchain.

The Master Right: You retain 100% ownership of your masters. Otto does not take your copyright; we simply provide the rails for it to travel.

2.2 The AI Co-Pilot (The Hybrid Creator)
For the visionary, the producer, the experimenter. The Otto AI engine is not a replacement; it is an instrument.

Collaborative Synthesis: Use Otto’s AI to generate a drum loop, a bassline, or a vocal harmony to accompany your guitar.

Transparent Tagging: The system automatically tags which parts are Human and which are AI. This ensures honesty and transparency for the listener.

3.0 THE "DNA" ENGINE: Attribution & Earnings
This is the heart of the revolution. In the old world, tracking who owns what is a mess. On Otto, every song has a "Digital DNA" that tracks every contributor automatically.

3.1 The "Smart Split" Standard
Every song on Otto is a "Smart Contract." This means the money is split the second a song generates revenue. No waiting 6 months for a check.

Example Scenario:

The Song: "Neon Nights"

The Revenue: $1,000 generated.

The Split (Automatic):

35% to the Singer (Human)

35% to the Producer (Human)

10% to the AI Model Pool (Attributed to the developers/trainers of the AI tool used)

20% to the Otto DAO (Community Treasury)

3.2 The AI Attribution Protocol
We solve the "Stolen Voice" problem. If you use Otto's AI to emulate a specific style or sound:

The AI model used is cited in the credits.

A fraction of the earnings goes back to the training pool of that model. This ensures that as AI grows, the people who built the foundation also benefit.

4.0 THE COVER REVOLUTION: Remix. Cover. Earn.
In the traditional industry, releasing a cover song is a legal nightmare. On Otto, it is a one-click feature. We treat Covers and Remixes as a vital part of the culture.

4.1 The "Open Mic" License
Permissionless Creativity: You do not need to ask permission to cover a song on Otto. The permission is built into the protocol.

The "Cover" Logic: When you upload a cover of an existing Otto track, the system links your song to the original.

4.2 How Everyone Earns on a Cover
Let’s say User A (The Fan) creates a techno cover of a ballad by User B (The Star).

Composition Royalty: User B (The Star) automatically receives 50% of the earnings from User A's cover, because they wrote the melody/lyrics.

Performance Royalty: User A (The Fan) keeps 50% for their new production and performance.

Result: The original artist wants you to cover their song because it makes them money. The cover artist gets exposure and fair pay.

5.0 THE GLOBAL STAGE: How You Get Heard
We don't rely on a secret algorithm. We rely on The People.

5.1 The Tribunal (Community Curation)
The "Tribunal" is our version of a record label's A&R department—but run by you.

Scouting: Listeners scout for hidden gems in the "New Arrivals" feed.

Voting: The community votes on the best tracks. High-quality tracks are "Upvoted" to the global main stage.

Reward: If you vote for a song early and it becomes a hit, you earn points/tokens. You are rewarded for your "Golden Ear."

5.2 Universal Distribution
Otto is the launchpad.

Internal: Your music lives on the Otto Decentralized App (dApp) for the community.

External: With one click, successful tracks are pushed to Spotify, Apple Music, and other DSPs. The royalties from these outside platforms flow back into your Otto wallet.
